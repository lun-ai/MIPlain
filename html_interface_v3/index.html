Introduction

Do you want to become a Noughts and Crosses wizard?
To become a Noughts and Crosses wizard, you need to pass a trial. This trial includes 3 parts.
You will answer some questions. And you will become an apprentice of the Great Wizard.
Finally, you will apply what you learn by answering some more questions.


Here are some basics of becoming a Noughts and Crosses wizard.
Noughts and Crosses or Tic-tac-toe, is a two-players game for X and O,
who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid.
You can make a move in a untaken cell when the game has not terminated.

A TRIPLE-LINE is an alignment of three of the same mark. The player who succeeds in placing
their marks in a HORIZONTAL, VERTICAL, or DIAGONAL TRIPLE-LINE wins the game.
Each highlighted line of cells in the following board states is an example of TRIPLE-LINE.

A DOUBLE-LINE is an alignment of two of the same mark. There can be multiple DOUBLE-LINES for a player.
Each highlighted line of cells in the following board states is an example of DOUBLE-LINE.

A player could have no TRIPLE-LINE or DOUBLE-LINE. Following examples highlight such cases.
