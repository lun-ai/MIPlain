Here are some basics of becoming a Noughts and Crosses wizard.
Noughts and Crosses or Tic-tac-toe, is a two-players game for X and O,
who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid.
You can make a move in a untaken cell when the game has not terminated.

their marks in a HORIZONTAL, VERTICAL, or DIAGONAL TRIPLE-LINE wins the game.
